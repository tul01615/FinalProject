Homicides vs. Airbnbs in Chicago

The problem at hand when creating these maps was to describe the spatial distribution of the density of homicides by police district and compare this spatial distribution to Airbnb listings and prices in Chicago. In order to accomplish this, joins had to be created to merge standalone data with spatial data. The data also had to be summarized to determine the frequency needed to map density. Using the area and the frequency, the density for both homicides and Airbnb's can be calculated separately. The maps show that not many Airbnb's are in areas within high-density homicide districts.
